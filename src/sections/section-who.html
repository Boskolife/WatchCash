Meet Dan The founder of WatchCash

At WatchCash, we believe every timepiece tells a story – and every client deserves a partner they can trust. Since our founding, we’ve helped thousands of clients in the U.S. and Canada buy and sell pre-owned luxury watches quickly, securely, and with complete peace of mind.

With offices in Miami and Toronto, our team of expert watchmakers, appraisers, and client advisors combines deep horological knowledge with a personal, concierge-style approach. Whether you’re selling a Rolex Submariner or buying a Patek Philippe, we handle each transaction with the care it deserves.
